About Samsung

A Legacy of Innovation

Founded in 1938, Samsung began as a small trading company before evolving into one of the world's leading technology corporations spanning electronics, appliances, and mobile devices.

For over eight decades, Samsung has been at the forefront of technological advancement, redefining what's possible through relentless innovation and unwavering commitment to quality. From our groundbreaking smartphone designs to revolutionary display technology, we remain dedicated to enriching people's lives through meaningful innovation.
